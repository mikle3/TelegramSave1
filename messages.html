Реализация:

Нарисуйте или распечатайте картину (например, Снежную Королеву, Гринча или зимний пейзаж) и разрежьте её на несколько частей.

Задание:
Игроки должны "разморозить" картину, извлекая её кусочки и собрав всё вместе. Когда картина будет собрана, на ней будет изображён символ или подсказка для следующего шага.

Problem 1: Who sent the letter?
After the death of Grinch, the Snow Queen found three letters, each of them could be sent by either Santa, Grinch or Jack. In the letters there were the following phrases:
- Letter One: "I can't let this go on, everything will end soon."
- Letter Two: "If he did not intervene, everything would be different."
- Letter Three: "I fulfill the orders because I have to."
Who of them sent the letter if:
a) Santa will never directly acknowledge his actions.
b) Jack always follows orders.
c) Grinch blames Santa in all his misfortunes.
Answer: 1-Santa, 2-Grinch, 3-Jack

Problem 2: Who tells the truth?
After Santa's house was frozen, Santa, Jack and the Snow Queen have given some evidence:
- Santa: "I have nothing to do with the murder of Grinch. Jack did it of his own will."
- Jack: "I just fulfilled the order, however the order was not deadly."
- Snow Queen: "I know, who killed Grinch and it was Santa."
If only one of them is true, who is the killer?
Amswer: Jack.

Problem 3: Secret Messages
Santa found out about the secret messages between the Snow Queen and Grinch. In one of the messages Grinch writes:
"In three days at midnight meet me where ice and snow meet the stars."
Santa sends Jack to the assigned place, but it turns out to be a trap. Jack is freezing in the ice cold cave. On which day of the week did Grinch send the message if Jack came to the cave at midnight on Friday?
Answer: Tuesday

Problem 4: Code from the Ice Chest
In Santa's frozen house you find a chest, locked with a code lock. It says on the chest: "To open me add the days of the winter, the feast of love and the starry nights of December."
Answer: 136 (Because the winter is the 2024 winter)

Problem five: The Order
You have four colours in front of you: Red, Green, Yellow, Blue.
The sign on the wall says (we will actually put a sign there) "The Red behind the Green. Blue not with Yellow. The Green in front of all."
Answer: Green Yellow Red Blue.
